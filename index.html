CARISCA 2024 SUMMIT

Date: June 4-6, 2024

Venue: Blu Hotel, Nairobi Upper Hill, Kenya

"Africa’s Supply Chain Resilience for Economic Transformation"

Read More

About the Summit

We are pleased to announce that the fourth annual Supply Chain Research Summit is set for June 4-6, 2024, with a pre-conference Paper Development Workshop on June 3. Both will be held in Nairobi, Kenya, at the . The summit will be hybrid. You can attend in person or online.




All Roads to Kenya in June, 2024

On June 20-22, 2023, CARISCA hosted the third annual Supply Chain Research Summit in Ghana. The summit attracted over 600 participants from 59 countries and featured more than 90 presenters.
In 2024, we are moving the summit to Kenya's capital, Nairobi, to accommodate even more supply chain scholars, students and professionals. As in 2023, the 2024 Summit will be a hybrid event, held in person at the Radisson Blu Hotel, Nairobi, Kenya, and virtually via Zoom.

Call for Papers

This year’s summit will take an in-depth look at “Africa’s Supply Chain Resilience for Economic Transformation.” We invite you to share your research, knowledge and insights by submitting a paper abstract for presentation at the summit.

Learn more

PhD Dissertation Award competition

The summit will include a PhD Dissertation Award competition, as it has in prior years. This competition is meant to showcase the best logistics and supply chain management PhD research projects being carried out in African higher education institutions.
Finalists will present their research at the summit and be recognized with a certificate and stipend.

Learn more

Paper Development Workshop

Faculty and senior doctoral candidates in higher education institutions in Africa are invited to participate in the pre-conference Paper Development Workshop. Selected applicants will meet with and receive professional advice from senior supply chain scholars on their submitted academic research paper.
Participants can get individual feedback on their research paper in a safe environment and engage with senior colleagues in focused discussions.
